  • Purdue Polytechnic Institute
  • Veterinary Technology

Guests and Dining

Because of limited capacity in the academic-department-level activities, each student will be limited to no more than two registered guests. Lunch in a student dining court will be free for the student and up to two guests; if other guests accompany the student to lunch, they will be required to pay.
